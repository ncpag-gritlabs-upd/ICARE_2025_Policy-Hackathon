# SCORING RUBRIC — ICARE 2025 POLICY HACKATHON

Judges will evaluate entries using the following criteria:

# 1. Problem Definition & Data Analysis (30 points)

**Excellent (22–25):**  
Shows exceptional precision in identifying a governance problem, grounded firmly in patterns observed in the dataset.

**Good (19–21):**  
Clear problem definition and reasonable interpretation of trends.


# 2. Proposed Solutions (30 points)

**Excellent (22–25):**  
Highly feasible, evidence-based recommendations with clear understanding of administrative, technical, social, and political dimensions.


# 3. Innovation & Originality (15 points)

**Excellent (14–15):**  
Novel, creative, context-appropriate solutions that respond directly to data signals.



# 4. Presentation (15 points)

**Excellent (14–15):**  
Clear, engaging, effective visual presentation with correct use of data and compelling narrative.



# 5. Q&A Performance (10 points)

**Excellent (9–10):**  
Confident, data-backed responses demonstrating deep understanding of methods and assumptions.
